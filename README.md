### How to set-up?
1. Open your VSCode
2. Press `CTRL + Shift + X` or `CMD + Shift + X` to open extensions menu
3. Search for `dbf` or `dbf-publisher`
4. Click to **install**.
5. Press F1 and type `DBF | Turn ON`
